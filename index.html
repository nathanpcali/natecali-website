About

An LA native, Nate Cali has overseen features, digital content, and commercials. He has edited campaigns for clients including Nike, Levi's, Walmart, Funny or Die, and Apple. More recently, his work has focused on international automotive brands including Ford, Dodge, Maserati, Audi, RAM, Jeep, Chevy, and BMW. Prior to joining Harbor, Cali worked at numerous edit shops, most recently Nomad and The Den.
